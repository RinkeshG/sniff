Social · Product
"Sniff-approved" seal
A clean mark of approval — stamps genuinely good foods in the report, and works as a shareable sticker.
Social
Peeking cat
Peers over the bottom edge of share/OG cards and Insta-story frames — instant "Sniff was here." Also a sweet bottom-of-page sticker.
Fun · New pose
Cat loaf
The internet's favourite cat shape. A sticker, a "loading slowly / take it easy" state, or a quiet footer flourish. Pure brand affection.
Fun
"…oops" knock-off
A cat batting a kibble off the edge — the perfect 404 / something-broke screen. "The cat knocked this off the table. Try again."
Experimental
Slow blink
In cat language a slow blink means "I trust you." A success micro-moment — after a search lands, or on the waitlist confirm: the cat slow-blinks at you.
Experimental
Self-drawing cat
The cat's line draws itself on, in one continuous gesture — a hero entrance or an in-between-states animation. The brand, sketching live.
